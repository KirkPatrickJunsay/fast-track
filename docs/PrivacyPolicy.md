# Privacy Policy — Fast Track

_Last updated: May 19, 2026_

Fast Track is built privacy-first. Your fasts, weights, moods, water and badges live on your device only. There are no accounts, no servers, and no third-party trackers.

## Local-only by design

Everything you log is stored in an encrypted SQLite database inside Fast Track's private app sandbox on your phone. Nothing is uploaded to a server, shared with us, or sold to anyone — because there is no server in the loop.

## What we collect

We collect **nothing**. The app itself has no analytics SDK, no crash reporting service, and no advertising identifier. The data you see in History, Insights and Trophies was all generated by you, on this device, and never leaves it unless you choose to export it.

## Permissions

Fast Track only requests permissions it actually uses:

- **Notifications** — to remind you about fasting-stage milestones and quiet hours. Local-only, scheduled by the OS.
- **Storage** — to write your export JSON to a location you pick when you tap "Export".
- **Vibration** — a subtle pulse on long-press and swipe-to-delete. Off if your phone is in silent mode.

## Your data, your control

Because the data lives on your device, you have full control:

- **Export** — Settings → Data → Export creates a complete JSON snapshot you own.
- **Import** — restore from any previous export to move to a new phone.
- **Reset** — Settings → Data → Reset all data wipes every row from the database and returns you to onboarding.

Uninstalling Fast Track also removes the database with it.

## Medical disclaimer

Fast Track is a personal logging tool, **not a medical device**. It does not diagnose, treat or prevent any condition. Intermittent fasting may not be appropriate if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, under 18, managing diabetes, have a history of disordered eating, or are taking medication that requires food. Please talk to a healthcare professional before starting or changing a fasting routine.
